About Us

Securicore

SecuriCORE is operating in the field of integrated security management and supplies since 2002, supported by UNIDO (United Nations Industrial Development and Organization) as well as the Government of Bahrain, represented by the Ministry of Industry and Commerce and BDB (Bahrain Development Bank).

SecuriCORE provides both Manned Security Services and Electronic Security Systems with advanced security solutions as total integrated security solution for its clients, simultaneously offering them security consultancy for round the clock, peace of mind by reducing the risk of theft, vandalism, harm, sabotage and escape.

SecuriCORE management has an extensive background in law enforcement in the Kingdom of Bahrain and private security service whose expertise in operating the Security Service has been passed on to the entire Team.
